Frequently Asked Questions
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.
How can I view my grades?
Your grades are available in the 'Grades / Results' section of the dashboard sidebar.
How do I contact my mentor?
Your mentor's contact information can be found in the 'Mentor' section of the dashboard sidebar.
